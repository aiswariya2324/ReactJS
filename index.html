Classic British Fish and Chips Recipe

Ingredients of Fish Fingers

500 gm fish fillets2 tablespoon all purpose flour
salt as required2 tablespoon parmesan cheese
1 tablespoon mayonnaise1 teaspoon black pepper
2 lemon wedges 1 egg
2 tablespoon refined oiltartare sauce as required
parsley as required1 1/2 cup panko breadcrumbs

How to make Fish Fingers

Step 1 Roast breadcrumbs

Preheat the oven at 180 degree Celsius. Spread breadcrumbs on a baking tray with cooking oil sprayed on it. Roast them until golden for 3-4 minutes. Transfer these golden breadcrumbs into a bowl and add parmesan cheese, salt and black pepper to taste. Mix well and keep aside.

Step 2 Prepare the batter for coating

Increase the oven temperature to 220 degrees Celsius. Next, spray another baking tray with cooking oil. Then, take a bowl and mix egg, mayonnaise, all-purpose flour and, salt and pepper to prepare the batter.

Step 3 Coat fish in batter and bake

Cut the fish fillets into strips of 1.5 x 11 cm. Dip the fillets into the batter and pick it up with tongs and toss them in the breadcrumbs to coat properly. Transfer to the greased baking tray and spray with a little refined oil. Place the tray in the preheated pan and bake for 12 minutes or until crispy on the outside.

Step 4 Serve fish fingers

Serve the fish fingers with tartare sauce and garnish with lemon wedges and chopped parsley
